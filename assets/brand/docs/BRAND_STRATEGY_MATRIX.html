3 Logo & Iconography

Current problems: Generic (any electrical company anywhere), "LOGISTICS SERVICES" is wrong and confusing, no connection to Los Cabos, the teal IS the Bobo's feet color but nobody knows.

Why "Logistics Services" Must Change — For Salo

The problem: When a potential client sees "LOGISTICS SERVICES" under the logo, they think: shipping company, freight, transportation. Not energy. Not solar. Not what we sell.

The cost: Every confused visitor is a lost lead. If even 1 in 10 bounces because they think "wrong company", that's real money lost every month.

The fix: Change to "ENERGY SOLUTIONS". Three words that tell every visitor exactly what we do. Zero ambiguity. The logo shape, colors, and "Energy Cabo" name all stay. Only the subtitle changes.

Who advised this? Unknown — likely a generic template. It doesn't reflect what the company does and never has.

The "300 Days of Sun" Concept

Los Cabos has ~300 sunny days/year — one of the highest solar irradiance levels in North America. This single fact justifies investment, differentiates from cloudy regions, creates urgency, works everywhere (web, proposals, social, conversations), and is verifiable — not marketing fluff.

Key Takeaways

  • Photography: Cabo golden-hour light on luxury homes (Montage/Waldorf), NOT generic stock solar panels
  • Color: Desert golds + ocean blues + clean whites = our BCS palette already matches
  • Typography: Clean sans-serif primary (Tesla/Apple), optional serif accent for luxury (Montage)
  • Hero: Full-bleed video or photo of a Cabo luxury home with energy system, single clear CTA
  • Narrative: Sustainability + luxury lifestyle fusion (Rivian mission + Tesla minimalism)
  • Avoid: Cluttered layouts, green "eco" cliches, generic solar panel stock photos
