## 3. LOGO & ICONOGRAPHY

### Current logo

The current logo: electrical plug/bolt inside a circle, "Energy Cabo" text, "LOGISTICS SERVICES" subtitle. Navy + teal + white.

**Problems identified**:
- Generic — could be any electrical company anywhere
- "LOGISTICS SERVICES" is wrong and actively confusing — Energy Cabo is energy solutions, not a shipping company
- No connection to Los Cabos identity
- The teal IS the Bobo de patas azules color, but nobody knows that

### Why "Logistics Services" must change — argument for Salo

**The problem**: When a potential client searches "Energy Cabo" and sees "LOGISTICS SERVICES" under the logo, they think: shipping company, freight, transportation. Not energy. Not solar. Not what we sell. This creates confusion at the most critical moment — the first impression.

**The cost**: Every confused visitor is a lost lead. If even 1 in 10 visitors bounces because they think "wrong company", that's real money lost every month. The estimator, the proposals, the WhatsApp — none of it matters if they leave before scrolling.

**The fix**: Change to "ENERGY SOLUTIONS". Three words that tell every visitor exactly what Energy Cabo does. Zero ambiguity. This isn't a rebrand — it's a correction. The logo shape, colors, and "Energy Cabo" name all stay the same. Only the subtitle changes.

**Who advised this?** Unknown, but it was likely a generic template from whoever made the logo. It doesn't reflect what the company actually does and never has.

### Logo evolution options

- [ ] **Option A — Minimal refresh**: Keep current shape, remove "LOGISTICS SERVICES", add "Energy Solutions". Quick, low risk.
- [ ] **Option B — Colibri de Xantus**: New icon based on the hummingbird endemic to BCS. Nobody else can use this. Strongest differentiator.
- [ ] **Option C — Bobo patas azules**: Icon referencing the blue-footed booby. Connects teal color to the bird. Fun, memorable.
- [ ] **Option D — Los Arcos silhouette**: The most recognizable landmark of Cabo. Every client knows it. Instant location recognition.
- [ ] **Option E — Abstract sun + wave**: Solar energy + ocean. Modern, clean, works at any size.
- [ ] **Option F — Cardón cactus**: Symbol of BCS resilience. "We're rooted here." Works well with the product name "El Cardon."

### Icon set for services/products

- [ ] **Fauna icons**: Each service gets a BCS animal icon (Bobo, Colibri, Mantarraya, Ballena, Cardenal)
- [ ] **Geography icons**: Each service gets a BCS landmark (Arcos, Pulmo, Sierra Laguna, Medano, Todos Santos)
- [ ] **Abstract energy icons**: Modern line icons (sun, battery, shield, grid) — safe, universal
- [ ] **Hybrid**: Product NAMES are BCS, but icons stay abstract (best of both worlds)

### The "300 days of sun" concept

This is Energy Cabo's most powerful selling argument. Los Cabos has approximately 300 sunny days per year — one of the highest solar irradiance levels in North America. This single fact:

- Justifies solar investment (ROI is almost guaranteed)
- Differentiates from companies in cloudy regions
- Creates urgency ("you're wasting 300 days of free energy")
- Works in every touchpoint: web, proposals, social, conversations
- Is verifiable and credible (not marketing fluff)
